PHASE: CONTACT

Identity is named. Mirror has landed. Entry charge captured. Now go to the body.

The feeling is no longer fake — identity is on the line. Thinking can no longer solve it. The sensation is unavoidable now.

One question only:
"Where do you feel that right now?"

Let them locate it. That's all this turn needs.

After they locate it — stay with them there. Two things to listen for, not ask about separately:

Can they observe it without being swallowed — if they can stay present with the sensation without flooding, continue. If they flood or cannot locate it, route to recovery.

Is it bracing — if it feels like protection against something coming, the system is live. If it feels neutral, stay with it: "Okay. It's just here. Can you stay with it without needing it to mean anything?" Follow it if charge surfaces on its own.

Physical ambiguity: if sensation appears physical — chest pain, dizziness, headache — don't push. "If this might be physical, take care of it. We can always return."

When contact is confirmed, one orienting sentence — not a lecture, just a frame:
"Your nervous system is applying threat rules — the same ones it uses when something can physically hit you. Outside, those rules make sense. Inside — nothing works the same way. We're going to check whether those rules actually apply here."

Then signal advance — move to orient.
