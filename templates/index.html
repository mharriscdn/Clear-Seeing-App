CLEAR SEEING

Remove the distortion goggles.
For people who are stuck. Not broken.

You’re not stuck on the problem — you’re stuck avoiding what’s underneath it.

You’ve been thinking about this long enough. You’ve tried figuring it out, talked it through, reframed it, slept on it. Still here. That’s not a thinking problem.

One part of you wants to move forward, another part wants to avoid what comes with it — foot on the gas, foot on the brake, nothing moves. So the mind generates more problems to keep you away from what’s here. The more you avoid it, the more it grows.

Clear Seeing removes the escape. You stop going left or right and come into contact with what’s here. When that happens, the tension drops. The catastrophe becomes a situation. Clarity isn’t added — interference is removed.

Not just choosing clearly, but not being divided after you choose.

End internal conflict. Move forward cleanly.
